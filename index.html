Privacy Policy for Neuma

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Neuma. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle information within the Neuma mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Neuma is designed to be a private experience. We collect minimal data required for account synchronization via Supabase, such as your email address and basic profile information.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

We use Google Play Services: For processing in-app purchases and managing subscriptions..

We use AdMob: To serve banner ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers to personalize ads (unless you disable personalization).

We have Data Retention: For free tier data, where it is stored locally on the device and is lost upon uninstallation. Cloud backup is a premium feature..

4. Account Deletion support

Neuma provides an automated way to delete your account. You can navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account within the app to permanently wipe your data. This process is instantaneous and removes your email, profile, and all saved affirmations from our servers.

3. Children's Privacy

Neuma does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.
